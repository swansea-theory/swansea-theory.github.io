Tonicha Crook passed her PhD viva

Congratulations to Tonicha Crook passed her PhD viva today. The examiners were Pieter Collins (Maastricht) and Faron Moller (Swansea). The title of her PhD thesis is:

Computable Analysis and Game Theory: From Foundations to Applications. The PhD was supervised by Arno Pauly.

The Swansea Theory Group is internationally renowned for its research in Logic in Computer Science. The active research areas are: Computability Theory, Computational Complexity, Proof Theory, Type Theory, Game Theory, Algorithms,

Formal Methods (Automated and Interactive Theorem Proving), Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, Verification of Railway Control Systems), Artificial Intelligence (Satisfiability Solving, Multi-agent Systems, Argumentation Theory, Trustable Machine Learning, AI and Law).
