date: 2024-03-28

Monika, Faron, Olga, Filippos, Iain and James are attending the 40th British Colloquium for Theoretical Computer Science ([BCTCS 2024](https://bctcs2024.github.io/)) hosted by the University of Bath. This year is it also collocated with the 5th Southern and Midlands Logic Seminar.

  

Monika has been invited to give a talk on Logic in Railway Verification. Among the other invited speakers are

[Anupam Das](https://www.anupamdas.com/)(University of Birmingham),

[Alex Kavvos](https://seis.bristol.ac.uk/~tz20861/)(University of Bristol), and

Stuart Matthews (Capgemini Engineering).
