The [Swansea Theory
Group](https://www.swansea.ac.uk/compsci/research-and-impact/theoretical-computer-science/)
is internationally renowned for its research in Logic in Computer
Science. The active research areas are: Computability Theory,
Computational Complexity, Proof Theory, Type Theory, Game Theory,
Algorithms, Formal Methods (Automated and Interactive Theorem
Proving), Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, Verification of
Railway Control Systems, Artificial Intelligence (Satisfiability
Solving, Multi-agent Systems, Argumentation Theory, Trustable Machine
Learning, AI and Law).
