<p>Welcome to <a href="https://swansea-theory.blogspot.com/p/about-us.html " target="_blank">Swansea University Theory Group</a>'s new blog. Follow us for updates!</p><p>The Swansea Theory Group is internationally renowned for its research
 in Logic in Computer Science. The active research areas are: 
Computability Theory, Computational Complexity, Proof Theory, Type 
Theory, Game Theory, Algorithms,&nbsp;Formal Methods (Automated and 
Interactive Theorem Proving), Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, 
Verification of Railway Control Systems), Artificial Intelligence 
(Satisfiability Solving, Multi-agent Systems, Argumentation Theory, 
Trustable Machine Learning, AI and Law).</p>
<p>The research on Railway Control Systems has lead to the formation of the <a href="https://www.swansea.ac.uk/compsci/research-and-impact/swansea-railway-verification/">Swansea Railway Verification Group</a>&nbsp;which also delivered&nbsp;two Impact Case Studies.</p>
<p>Another recently formed subgroup studies the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.swansea.ac.uk/compsci/research-and-impact/educational-historical-philosophical-foundations/">Educational, and Historical and Philosophical Foundation</a>&nbsp;of Computer Science.</p><p><br /></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><a href="https://swansea-theory.blogspot.com/p/about-us.html "><br /></a></p>
