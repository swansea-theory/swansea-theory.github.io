On this day, we commemorate Ludwig Wittgenstein's birthday. 
Born in 1889 in Vienna, Austria, Wittgenstein's profound 
influence on Swansea University and its philosophical discourse is 
remembered. 

Check [Dr Alan Sandry](https://www.swansea.ac.uk/staff/a.sandry/)'s [Am I Glad To Be Here!](https://collections.swansea.ac.uk/s/Swansea-2020/page/wittgenstein-in-swansea) to learn more about Ludwig Wittgenstein and Philosophy at Swanse.
